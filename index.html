CrystalCollector!

You will be given a random number to begin the game.


There will be four crystal below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each cystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts the values for each of the gems will be different.
